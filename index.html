Startup Timelines

I have always found the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine to be a highly underrated resource, despite how interesting it can be. It is humbling to see how simple many startups were when they first got started, and also how quickly many have grown.

I have taken screenshots of notable companies from the Internet Archive and collected some of the interesting pics onto a timeline, so that others can easily see each startup's evolution over time.

Although I have only included the homepages, I hope others can also find them interesting. If you do, please consider donating to the Internet Archive today. Click on any company below to see its timeline:
